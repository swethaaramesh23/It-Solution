Top 5 Cybersecurity Threats in 2024 and How to Prevent Them

In today’s fast-growing digital world, cybersecurity has become a critical part of every business. With increasing online activities, cyber threats are evolving rapidly.

Top 5 cybersecurity threats:

1. Phishing Attacks

Fake emails/messages used to steal sensitive information.

2. Ransomware

Locks systems and demands money.

3. Malware

Viruses and spyware that damage systems.

4. Insider Threats

Internal misuse or accidental data leaks.

5. Cloud Security Risks

Misconfigured cloud systems expose data.

How to Prevent These Threats

✔ Strong passwords & MFA
✔ Antivirus & firewall
✔ Regular updates
✔ Employee training
✔ Secure cloud setup
